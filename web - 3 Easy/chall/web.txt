Elias Carpenter was a quiet craftsman who built furniture, clocks, and secret compartments.
Before he passed away, he left behind a digital vault filled with old workshop files — blueprints, notes, and a few odd strings of letters.

Rumor says one of those strange strings hides his final signature, a secret left for anyone clever enough to find it.
Your task is to explore the workshop’s vault, uncover the clues, and decode the hidden message buried deep within the archive.

But be careful — the workshop’s preview system doesn’t behave like an ordinary viewer.
It sometimes reveals more than it should to anyone who knows how to ask the right way…
