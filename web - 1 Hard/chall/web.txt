A small family has just opened a mysterious online bakery called “Little Bakery”. Everyone thinks it’s just a simple cake delivery website, but rumors say Adam hides secret recipes that can only be accessed through the special endpoints of his site.

Players stumble upon the bakery’s public page and see some broken links. One link leads to a “Not Found” page, but something seems off — the error page looks almost real, yet the section glows in the dark… or maybe it’s just another portal or dark-mode.
